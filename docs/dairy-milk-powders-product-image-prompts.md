# Dairy & Milk Powders product-image prompts

Generated 16 July 2026 with the built-in image-generation workflow. Each product used a separate generation call and independent composition; selected outputs were converted to 800×1200 WebP with new `-light-v4` filenames. Earlier assets were retained.

## Shared production constraints

Use case: `product-mockup`. Photorealistic premium B2B dairy editorial photography in a 2:3 portrait composition. Keep the upper 38–42% calm, pale and readable for dark website overlay copy. Use a light ivory or cool neutral palette, realistic commercial scale and controlled studio daylight. No text, labels, logos, brands, watermarks, hands, people or consumer branding. Do not default to the repeated bowl-and-scoop composition used by the previous image family.
