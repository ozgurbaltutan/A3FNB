# Fats & Oils product-image prompts

Generated 15 July 2026 with the built-in image-generation workflow. The established Grains and Dairy product assets were supplied as visual references: `milling-wheat-light-v1.webp` and `butter-light-v3.webp`.

## Shared visual contract

Vertical commercial ingredient still life matching the supplied A3 reference images: warm neutral beige studio backdrop and surface, soft diffused daylight, restrained natural shadows, realistic food texture, premium but practical B2B sample photography, subject arranged in the lower two-thirds with generous clean negative space above. No people, text, labels, logos, trademarks, branded containers or decorative packaging. Each oil or fat must remain physically and visually distinct from the other seven assets.

## Asset prompts

- `sunflower-oil-light-v3.webp`: clear pale-gold sunflower oil in an unlabelled transparent glass carafe and a small tasting dish, with a restrained scatter of sunflower seeds and one sunflower head.
- `corn-maize-oil-light-v1.webp`: clear pale-gold maize-germ oil in an unlabelled glass carafe and shallow dish, with yellow maize kernels and a cut corn-cob detail as the crop cue.
- `olive-oil-light-v1.webp`: green-gold olive oil in an unlabelled clear glass bottle and tasting dish, with a few green and dark olives and olive leaves.
- `palm-oil-light-v1.webp`: distinctly semi-solid golden-orange palm oil in a plain ceramic sample bowl with a clean scoop mark, accompanied by a restrained oil-palm fruit cue; it must not resemble a clear liquid oil.
- `palm-olein-light-v1.webp`: distinctly transparent, pourable golden palm olein in an unlabelled glass carafe and shallow dish, accompanied by a restrained oil-palm fruit cue; it must not resemble the semi-solid palm-oil asset.
- `margarines-light-v1.webp`: pale-yellow margarine shown as a smooth rectangular brick and a small unlabelled open sample tub, visibly manufactured and spreadable rather than dairy butter.
- `bakery-pastry-fats-light-v1.webp`: pale functional bakery fat as a clean lamination sheet and shortening blocks, with a stainless pastry scraper as a restrained process cue and no finished pastry.
- `specialty-fats-light-v1.webp`: technical specialty-fat system shown as pale blocks, uniform flakes and a small liquid sample, arranged like supplier application-lab ingredients for coatings or fillings.

## Validation checklist

- Warm-neutral studio treatment and vertical crop align with the latest Grains and Dairy product cards.
- No legible text, labels, logos or branded packs.
- Oil colour and physical state are credible and the palm-oil/palm-olein pair is unmistakably different.
- Margarine is not presented or described as dairy butter.
- Product remains legible under the category-card overlay and responsive centre crop.
