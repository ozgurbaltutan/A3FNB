# Starches & Sweeteners product-image prompt and acceptance register

Generated and reviewed on 15 July 2026 with the built-in `imagegen` workflow. All accepted files are new 800 × 1200 WebP assets; previous dark category images were not overwritten.

## Shared art direction

Portrait 2:3 commercial ingredient sample; warm pale ivory/stone seamless background; soft natural light; restrained realistic colour; subject grouped in the lower half; upper 45–50% kept calm for card copy; unbranded stainless scoop, neutral dish or clear glass sample vessel as appropriate. No packaging, labels, text, logos, watermarks or people. Powders, crystals and liquids must retain a commercially credible physical form.
