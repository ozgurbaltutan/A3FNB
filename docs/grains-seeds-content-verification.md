## Technical language

- [Codex Standard for Wheat and Durum Wheat (CXS 199-1995)](https://www.fao.org/input/download/standards/62/CXS_199e.pdf) supports the commodity distinction and the use of moisture, test weight, defects and related quality criteria.
- [Codex Standard for Maize (Corn) (CXS 153-1985)](https://www.fao.org/input/download/standards/51/CXS_153e.pdf) supports whole-shelled maize terminology and the use of moisture, foreign matter and damaged/broken-kernel criteria.
- [USDA FGIS confectionery sunflower inspection guidance](https://www.ams.usda.gov/sites/default/files/media/FGIS9180_51.pdf) supports the distinction between confectionery sunflower assessment and a generic oilseed offer, including seed-size and kernel-condition considerations.

The page deliberately has no shared specification matrix. Wheat, durum, maize and sunflower seed do not have one common commercial grade system comparable to the sugar page’s ICUMSA presentation. Product criteria are therefore presented as selection and offer-confirmation points inside each modal.
