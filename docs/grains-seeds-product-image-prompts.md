# Grains & Seeds product-card image prompt set

The four cards were generated separately in **product-mockup mode** and prepared as 800 × 1200 WebP assets for the existing `category-overlay` card system. Every accepted output was reviewed for product identity and desktop/mobile crop safety.

## Shared art direction

Create a photorealistic editorial B2B ingredient product photograph in a 2:3 portrait composition. Use a seamless pale ivory to light stone background, leaving the upper half calm and uncluttered for a dark card-text overlay. In the lower half, place a shallow unbranded galvanized metal grain-sampling scoop or tray with the specified whole agricultural product and only restrained botanical context. Soft natural daylight from the upper left, muted harvest-neutral colours, realistic texture and scale, premium commodity-trade aesthetic, consistent camera height. No text, label, logo, packaging, watermark, person or hand.

## Milling Wheat

Show pale golden, physically accurate common-wheat kernels in the galvanized sampling scoop, with two or three light-golden common-wheat ears beside it. The kernels should be dry, plump and naturally varied. It must read as flour-milling wheat, not amber durum, barley, oats or a processed flour product.

Accepted file: `public/media/products/grains-seeds/milling-wheat-light-v1.webp`

## Durum Wheat for Pasta

Show deeper amber, hard and visibly vitreous durum-wheat kernels in the galvanized sampling scoop, with a few characteristic durum ears beside it. Emphasise the translucent amber density and angular hard-kernel appearance so it is unmistakably different from the paler milling-wheat card. No pasta, semolina or flour.

Accepted file: `public/media/products/grains-seeds/durum-wheat-light-v1.webp`

## Corn / Maize

Show only dry, mature yellow dent and flint maize kernels in the galvanized sampling scoop, with a restrained fragment of dried cob or husk at the edge. Kernels must be hard, irregular and commercially realistic. Do not show sweetcorn, fresh green husk, popcorn, cornmeal or processed food.

Accepted file: `public/media/products/grains-seeds/corn-maize-light-v1.webp`

## Sunflower Seed

Show clearly striped black-and-ivory in-shell sunflower seeds in the galvanized sampling scoop, with a small restrained dried sunflower-head fragment as context. Seeds must remain visibly in shell and commercially realistic. Do not show hulled kernels, roasted snack packaging, cooking oil or an oil bottle.

Accepted file: `public/media/products/grains-seeds/sunflower-seed-light-v1.webp`
