## Shared visual contract

Use case: `product-mockup`. Create a photorealistic premium B2B ingredient sample photograph in a 2:3 portrait composition. Use a seamless pale ivory-to-light-stone studio backdrop and matching surface. Concentrate the product in the lower half to lower two-thirds and preserve calm uncluttered negative space across the upper half for the card text overlay. Use soft diffused natural daylight from the upper left, restrained natural shadows, warm-neutral colour and physically accurate food texture and scale. Match the reference's background, camera height, negative-space balance and practical commodity aesthetic. No text, labels, logos, brands, packaging, watermark, people, hands, decorative tableware or unrelated foods.

## Accepted assets

- `raisins-sultanas-light-v1.webp`: galvanized ingredient scoop with clearly separated golden sultanas and dark brown raisins; seedless-looking, wrinkled, dry and naturally varied. No fresh grapes, cranberries, prunes or snack mix.
- `dried-apricots-light-v1.webp`: divided galvanized sample tray with credible muted golden-orange sulphured apricots and deeper amber-brown natural unsulphured apricots; whole and halved dried fruit only. No neon orange, fresh fruit or apricot kernels.
- `dates-light-v1.webp`: whole dried dates in natural amber-to-deep-brown variation with one pitted and one cut example showing the fibrous interior. No fresh dates, stuffing, syrup, religious props or dessert styling.
- `dried-figs-light-v1.webp`: pale tan-to-light-brown whole dried figs with flattened pear shapes, dry wrinkled skin and restrained natural sugar bloom, plus one split fig showing the seeded interior. No fresh purple figs or mould-like powder.
- `in-shell-pistachios-light-v1.webp`: beige in-shell pistachios, predominantly naturally split with muted green kernels and purple-brown skins, plus a restrained group of closed shells. Shelled kernels must not dominate.
- `pistachio-kernels-light-v1.webp`: shelled pistachio kernels in credible muted green, yellow-green and purple-brown skin tones, mostly whole with restrained pieces and peeled kernels. No shells, neon green, paste or dessert styling.
- `walnuts-light-v1.webp`: light-coloured walnut halves, quarters and pieces in a galvanized scoop with two or three intact English walnuts and one opened shell. No pecans, mixed nuts, rancid dark kernels or excessive crumbs.

## Validation checklist

- All seven cards use one coherent background, camera height and upper negative-space system.
- Dried fruit reads as dry and commercially handled, never fresh, candied or dessert-styled.
- Natural and sulphured apricots are visibly distinct without unrealistic neon colour.
- In-shell pistachios and pistachio kernels cannot be mistaken for the same product.
- Walnut kernels retain credible folded structure and the in-shell/kernel buying routes are both visible.
- No legible text, label, logo, branded pack or watermark is present.
